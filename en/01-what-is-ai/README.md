# Part One: A Worldview of AI Basics · Part Overview

> Before we start taking apart any complex machine, we always need to step back and get a look at its overall shape. This part is here to help you build a "big-picture worldview" of AI.

## Why Start With a "Worldview"

When learning anything new, the biggest trap is diving straight into the details—ending up seeing the trees but missing the forest. Learning AI is no different.

So we won't rush into algorithms or formulas. Part One does just one thing: **help you set up an overall framework for AI in your mind.** With that framework in place, you can keep filling in knowledge later without getting dizzy.

Here's an analogy: it's like moving to a new city. On day one, you don't rush to memorize the name of every little alley. Instead, you first get a sense of the big picture—roughly what the city looks like, what districts it has, how the subway connects them. **This part takes you on a first pass over the "map" of the AI city.**

## What You'll Read in This Part

Over three chapters, we answer three of the most basic—and most important—questions:

| Chapter | The Question It Answers | What You'll Gain |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Chapter 1** What AI Actually Is | What exactly is AI? How is it different from ordinary software? | A one-line grasp of AI's essence: a computer that can "learn" |
| **Chapter 2** A Brief History of AI | It's been at this for decades—so why did it explode in just the last few years? | An understanding of the "right timing and conditions" behind AI's rise |

## By the End of This Part, You'll Be Able To

- Explain "what AI is" to a friend in plain language, instead of vaguely saying "it's just a really smart computer."
- Understand why AI didn't appear out of nowhere but is a "grand eruption" after decades of accumulation.
- Walking down the street or scrolling on your phone, point out anytime: "Look, that's AI at work behind the scenes."
