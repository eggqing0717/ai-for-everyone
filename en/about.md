# About This Book

## Project Background

*AI for Everyone* was born from a simple idea: AI is profoundly changing the world, yet quality popular-science content about how AI works is often buried under mathematical formulas and technical jargon. We wanted to make a book that "everyone can understand"—one that explains the underlying principles of AI clearly, using the plainest language and the most everyday analogies.

## Our Mission

**To let every ordinary person truly understand how AI thinks.**

Not to memorize a few terms, but to genuinely "get it" from the heart—to understand why machines can recognize images, hold conversations, and create, to understand their strengths, and to understand their limitations as well.
